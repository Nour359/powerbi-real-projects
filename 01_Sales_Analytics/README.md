# Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Business Objective
Analyze sales performance and identify growth opportunities.

## Data Source
- Public retail sales dataset (CSV)
- Time range: 2 years

## Business Questions
- Which products generate the highest profit?
- Which regions are underperforming?
- Monthly revenue trends

## KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Sales Growth %

## Power BI Features Used
- Star Schema
- Time Intelligence (YTD, MoM)
- DAX Measures
- Drill-through
- Tooltips

## Insights
- Product A generates high revenue but low margin
- Region X shows consistent decline
